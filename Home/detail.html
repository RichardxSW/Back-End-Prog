Delicious fried rice recipes are always perfect for breakfast, lunch or dinner. Topped with eggs, shredded chicken, prawns, beef meatballs and fish balls, make this special fried rice recipe your new family favorite.

Fried Rice

6 Ingredients
10 Minutes
267 Calories
Lunch

Ingredients
for 1 Serving

  • Rice
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • Sweet Soy Sauce
  • Salt
  • White Pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat oil over medium heat.
  2. Saute spices until fragrant.
  3. Add rice and egg, stir until smooth.
  4. Add sweet soy sauce, stir again until smooth.
  5. Remove from heat and serve with complementary fried rice.
